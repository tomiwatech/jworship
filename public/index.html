About Just Worship

Friends, There can be no argument or doubt about it: these are the days of His power and an outpouring of His Spirit. More than ever before this year, we have witnessed the beginnings of a revival, a hunger and thirst for God and for the beauty of His presence. These are the days of revelation by the Holy Spirit, increased knowledge and understanding of the Lord and grace to soar like eagles. In these spiritually charged times, what better way is there to end a year and begin a new one than in reckless worship?

This year once again, people of different tribes, tongues, and nations will gather in one place to lift up songs of worship in one accord. On that night, we will not ask for anything, but we will offer all our hearts and all our love to the only One who is deserving of it, to the Audience of One. On Friday, November 17, 2017, join us at Grenadines Open Ground, Landmark Road, off Ligali Ayorinde Street, Victoria Island, Lagos. From 8pm till dawn, a divine encounter will unfold: our God will be “Revealing His Glory”. We are expectant. We are ready. Are you?

It's going to be a Night of Worship!.
